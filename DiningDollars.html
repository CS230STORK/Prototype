Information

  • Easy to use

    Dining Dollars, as well as Meal Plans, are kept track off on your personal student id. Making it very easy to use. There is no need to carry around multiple cards or cash now because it can all be done from the convience of simply swiping your student id card.

  • All students have access to Dining Dollars

    Every student that is enrolled in Morgantown College is eligible for Dining Dollars. Dining Dollars are only available to students of Morgantown College.

  • Anyone can fund a students Dining Dollars

    Anybody can add money to anybodies Dining Dollars account. This makes it very easy for Parents or relatives to give students money and know that it will be spent appropriately and responsibly.

  • Can be used all around campus

    Dining Dollars can be used just like cash at many different stores. They can be used at all the food vendors that accept our new Meal Plans , as well as other stores located on Morgantown College's campus.'

  • Unlimited access

    Dining Dollars can be used anytime throughout the day. There are no time frames that limit the use of Dining Dollars, Other than of course the hours of the store.

  • Your balance is always available

    Dining Dollars allow you to easily login and check your current balance. All you will need is your student email and your password.

  • Keep the money you dont spend

    Theres no need to worry about losing the money you don't spend. Dining Dollars carry over from semester to semester! Meaning if you have a remaining balance at the end of the spring semester, that money will still be there for the summer semester and even the fall semester.
