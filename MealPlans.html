Morgantown College is happy to announce that we now offer Meal Plans to Students, faculty, and even the general public! Students no longer have to eat in their dorms food facility! Meals Plans work at all campus food locations as well as certain third party restaurants. See below for more information.

General Information

  • EVERYONE is eligible for a meal plan

    With our new meal plans everyone is eligibile for a meal plan. Students and faculty can use their id cards for meal plans. The general public can request an id card that will hold their meal plan information on it.

  • Get a meal plan tailored to your needs

    We understand that no two people are the same, so why should their meal plans be the same? We now offer a variety of meal plans so that you dont have to waste food or money.

  • Flexibility with changing meal plans

    Not sure which meal plan is best for you? Thats okay because we allow you to change your meal plan at any time in the semester! However, you are only able to upgrade your meal plan and cannot downgrade. Unfortunately if your meal plan is too large you can downgrade at the end of the semester when you choose your new meal plan package.

  • Rollovers

    Unfortunately we are unable to allow meals to roll over from day to day as well as semester to semester. So if you have a three meal-a-day plan and only use two of those meals, the next day you cannot get four meals, you can still only get three.

Students

  • Getting a Meal Plan

    Since students members already have an id card, getting a meal plan is very simple. All that you have to do is fill out the meal plan form and once your done your id card will be ready to go.

General Public

  • Getting a Meal Plan

    In order to get a meal plan you have to contact card services and order a id card with 'general public' status. Then you can go and select a meal plan that fits your needs and fill out the meal plan form and your id card will do the rest.
